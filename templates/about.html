About NIE

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Shange

Founder & Head Teacher

With over 11 years of experience in education, Mr. Shange established Ntshofo Institution of Excellence (NIE) with a vision to provide quality and accessible education to students in the community. Passionate about Mathematics and dedicated to student success, he has helped thousands of learners achieve their academic goals. His commitment to excellence has even earned him international recognition, as he travelled overseas to receive an educational award for his outstanding contribution to student development.

Mr. Shange’s teaching philosophy focuses on understanding each student’s unique learning style and creating a supportive environment where every learner can thrive.

"Education is not just about passing exams; it's about building confidence, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning." -Mr Shange
